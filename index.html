Atari Breakout

By: Jonathan Heasley

How To Play:

  • When you begin a ball will be bouncing around
  • Bounce the ball off the platform by moving it with your mouse
  • The platform will follow your mouse left and right no need to click
  • The Goal is to break all of the blocks before you lose all 3 Lives
  • This is just like the classic except there are no upgrades available
  • Hope You Enjoy
